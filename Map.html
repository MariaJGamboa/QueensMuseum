Choose your narrative

We decided to incorporate two different ways in witch our visitors can explore our exhibition, the narratives are:

Historical: It follows the chronological order of the objects related to the respected queen.

Queens in Popular Culture: It follows how each queen’s personal choices (fashion, image, media presence) shaped and were shaped by popular culture.
